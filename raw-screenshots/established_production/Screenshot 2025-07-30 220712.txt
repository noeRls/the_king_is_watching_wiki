Sawmill (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 0.80s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Wood per cycle
- Description: Produces wood.

Build Cost:
- 80 Water

Special Effects:
- Sawmill production: Boosts production by 25%
- Friendly lumberjacks: Provides a 20% boost to the production of neighboring tiles

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Tree
- Same Cycle as basic version but much higher Capacity (1000 vs 200)
- Much higher water cost (80 vs 25) for construction
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Provides area-of-effect bonus to nearby production buildings (20% boost to neighbors)
- The neighboring tile bonus makes placement strategy important for maximum efficiency
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Acts as both a production building and a support building for nearby structures
