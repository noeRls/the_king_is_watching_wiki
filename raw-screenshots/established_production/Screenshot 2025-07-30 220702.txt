Wheat Field (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 0.80s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Wheat per cycle
- Description: Produces wheat.

Build Cost:
- 80 Water

Special Effects:
- Farm production: Boosts production by 30%
- Gold diggers: 25% chance of receiving extra 1 Gold with each production cycle

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Small Wheat Field
- Same Cycle as basic version but much higher Capacity (1000 vs 200)
- Much higher water cost (80 vs 25) for construction
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Provides bonus gold income with 25% chance per cycle
- The "Gold diggers" effect suggests workers sometimes find gold while farming
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Combines agricultural production with potential economic bonus
