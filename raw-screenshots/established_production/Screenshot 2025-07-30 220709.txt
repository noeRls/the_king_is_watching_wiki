Crystal Mine (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 1s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Crystal per cycle
- Description: Produces crystals.

Build Cost:
- 55 Wood
- 25 Stone
- 25 Food

Special Effects:
- Magic aura: Passively increases spell damage dealt by 5%
- Crystal Mine production: Boosts production by 30%

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Crystal Mine
- Higher construction cost and longer build time than basic version
- Higher Capacity (1000 vs 200)
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Provides passive magical enhancement to spell damage
- The magic aura effect makes this valuable for magical warfare and spellcasting units
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Combines resource production with magical combat benefits
