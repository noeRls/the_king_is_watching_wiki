Vineyard (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 1s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Grapes per cycle
- Description: Produces grapes.

Build Cost:
- 50 Wood
- 30 Water

Special Effects:
- Vineyard production: Boosts production by 30%
- Grape varieties: Each active vineyard provides 5 morale

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Grape Bushes
- Higher construction cost and longer build time than basic version
- Higher Capacity (1000 vs 200)
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Provides significant morale bonus (5 per vineyard) to your kingdom
- The morale benefit makes this valuable beyond just resource production
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Combines agricultural production with population happiness benefits
