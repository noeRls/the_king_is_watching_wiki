Clay Mine (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 1s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Clay per cycle
- Description: Produces clay.

Build Cost:
- 30 Wood
- 40 Food

Special Effects:
- Zero waste: 10% chance of repairing your castle for 1 HP with each production cycle
- Clay Mine production: Boosts production by 35%

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Clay Mine
- Higher Capacity (1000 vs 200) and longer build time (1s vs 0.80s)
- More expensive construction cost compared to basic version
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Includes beneficial special effects not present in basic version
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Provides both resource production and defensive benefits to your castle
