Iron Mine (Established Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 1s
- Capacity: 1000

Production:
- Produces: 1 Iron Ore per cycle
- Description: Produces ore.

Build Cost:
- 50 Food
- 25 Wood

Special Effects:
- Troop inspiration: Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of [Warrior] troops by 5%
- Iron Mine production: Boosts production by 30%

Additional Notes:
- This is an upgraded version of the basic Iron Mine
- Higher construction cost and longer build time than basic version
- Higher Capacity (1000 vs 200)
- No longer has "limited yield" - produces indefinitely
- Provides passive military enhancement specifically to Warrior-type troops
- The troop inspiration effect makes this valuable for military campaigns
- Part of the Established Production building category
- Combines resource production with military combat benefits
