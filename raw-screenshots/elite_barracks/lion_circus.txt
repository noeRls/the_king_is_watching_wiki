LION CIRCUS

Building Information:
- Cycle: 10s
- Capacity: 4

Requirements:
- 1 unit per cycle
- Consume 20 meat and 20 wheat per cycle

Function: Spawns Griffins

Build Cost:
- 100 meat
- 20 wheat

GRIFFIN UNIT STATS:

Base Characteristics:
- HP: 430
- Damage per second: 40
- Unit Types: [Rider] (orange circular icon), [Flying] (blue wings icon)

Unit Trait:
+2% to HP of Griffins for each Griffin on the battlefield.

UPGRADES:

Versatility:
- Griffins receive bonuses from all troop classes and are counted as all-class. Their cost to produce is increased by 100%
