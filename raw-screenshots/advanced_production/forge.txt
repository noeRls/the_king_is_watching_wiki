Forge (Advanced Production)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 1.50s

Production:
- Produces: 1 Metal per cycle
- Consumes: 1 Iron Ore per cycle (red minus symbol)
- Description: Smelts ore into metal.

Build Cost:
- 55 Gold
- 30 Ore

Upgrades:
- Efficient processing: Increases efficiency and speed. Now consumes 3 Iron Ore to produce 2 Metal
- Troop inspiration: Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of [Ranged] troops by 5%
