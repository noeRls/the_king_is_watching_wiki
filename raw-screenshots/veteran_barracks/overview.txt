VETERAN BARRACKS OVERVIEW

This shows the complete layout of the Veteran Barracks building complex, representing the second tier of military facilities with more advanced magical units:

Buildings Available:
- Academy of Nature (middle left position) - Produces Healer Mages
- Academy of Fire (bottom right position) - Produces Fire Mages
- Multiple unknown building slots (14 additional slots shown as question marks)

The Veteran Barracks focuses on magical/arcane units compared to the more basic physical troops from Levy Barracks:
- Healer Mages (support/healing arcane units)
- Fire Mages (offensive ranged arcane units)

This represents the intermediate military production facility in "The King Is Watching" game, offering access to specialized magical troops with enhanced abilities and support capabilities.
