Hospital
========

Cycle: 1s

Function/Description:
Heals your injured troops while active.

Build Costs:
- Stone: 280

Population Requirements:
Not specified in the screenshot

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Masters of healing: Heals 50% more
- Masters of morale: Each active hospital provides 4 morale

Additional Notes:
- Extremely fast Cycle (1 second)
- Essential for maintaining troop health during campaigns
- Provides both healing and morale benefits to your forces
- The healing bonus makes medical treatment significantly more effective
- Morale boost applies per active hospital, encouraging multiple constructions
- High stone cost reflects the importance and effectiveness of medical facilities
