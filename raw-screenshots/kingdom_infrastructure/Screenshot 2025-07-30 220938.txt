Magic College
=============

Cycle: 55s

Population Requirements: 45 (red cross icon)

Function/Description:
Generates basic spells.

Build Costs:
- Crystals: 250
- Wood: 250

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Magic College speed: Boosts spell production by 20%
- Illusion of choice: No longer generates random spells but grants spell choices instead

Additional Notes:
- Advanced version of the Magic School with higher capacity
- Requires significantly more population (45 vs 30) than Magic School
- Higher resource investment but better spell generation capabilities
- The "Illusion of choice" upgrade provides strategic control over spell acquisition
- More expensive but more efficient than the basic Magic School
