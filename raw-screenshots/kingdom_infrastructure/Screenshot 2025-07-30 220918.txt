Worshipper's Sanctuary (Kingdom Infrastructure)

Building Information:
- Cycle: 30s

Production:
- Sacrifices Black Sheep
- Receive permanent morale (1) for each sheep sacrificed

Build Cost:
- 100 Wood
- 90 Clay
- 30 Gold

Upgrades:
- Higher Morale: Boosts morale obtained from sacrificing the sheep by 1. Morale gained before the upgrade is unchanged.
- Production Speed: Boosts production by 25%
