WORSHIPPER'S SANCTUARY

Building Information:
- Cycle: 30s
- Capacity: Not specified

Function: Sacrifices Black Sheep. Receive permanent morale (1) for each sheep sacrificed.

Build Cost:
- 100 (red circular resource - appears to be food/supplies)
- 90 (tool/iron resource)
- 30 (bow/arrow resource)

ABILITIES:

Higher Morale:
- Boosts morale obtained from sacrificing the sheep by 1. Morale gained before the upgrade is unchanged.

Production Speed:
- Boosts production by 25%

This religious building provides a way to convert Black Sheep into permanent morale bonuses, offering long-term strategic benefits for kingdom management.
