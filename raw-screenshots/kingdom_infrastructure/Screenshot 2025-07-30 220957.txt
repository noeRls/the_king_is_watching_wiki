King's Statue
=============

Cycle: 1s

Population Requirements: 2 (red cross icon)

Function/Description:
Speeds up the King's Ability cooldown recovery while active.

Build Costs:
- Iron: 300

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Crystal clarity: 25% chance of receiving extra 1 crystal with each production cycle
- Troop inspiration: Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of [Champion] troops by 5%

Additional Notes:
- Instant construction (1 second build time)
- Focuses on enhancing the King's tactical abilities
- Provides economic benefit through crystal production bonus
- Military enhancement specifically benefits Champion-tier units
- High iron cost indicates this is a premium military/economic structure
- The statue serves both practical and inspirational purposes for the kingdom
