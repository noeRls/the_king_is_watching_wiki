Magic School
============

Cycle: 110s

Population Requirements: 30 (red cross icon)

Function/Description:
Slowly generates basic spells.

Build Costs:
- Wood: 50
- Stone: 40

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Magic School speed: Boosts spell production by 25%
- Illusion of choice: No longer generates random spells but grants spell choices instead

Additional Notes:
- Entry-level magical education building
- Provides controlled spell generation rather than random spell creation
- The "Illusion of choice" upgrade is particularly valuable for strategic spell selection
- Requires a moderate population investment to operate
