Execution Ground
===============

Cycle: 15s

Population Requirements: 1 (red cross icon, indicating population increase)

Function/Description:
Executes one of your [Grunt] units and provides Denarii for each unit.

Build Costs:
- Stone: 120

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- High bounties: Gain 2 extra per unit executed
- Troop inspiration: Passively increases damage dealt by and HP of [Grunt] troops by 5%

Additional Notes:
- Provides economic benefit through unit sacrifice
- The troop inspiration bonus affects all Grunt units, not just those being executed
- Quick Cycle for immediate strategic benefit
- Creates a trade-off between unit count and economic/military bonuses
- The population requirement likely represents the executioner or overseer needed
