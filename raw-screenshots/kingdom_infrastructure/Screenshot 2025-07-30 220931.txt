Tile Enchanter
==============

Cycle: 15s

Function/Description:
Enchants a tile with a 20% boost to production. Can be placed over existing buildings to enchant the tile instantly but will cost 50% more.

Build Costs:
- Wood: 100

Population Requirements:
Not specified in the screenshot

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Provides 20% production boost to the tile it's placed on
- Can enhance existing buildings by placement over them
- Instant enchantment capability for existing structures

Additional Notes:
- Quick Cycle makes it efficient for immediate improvements
- Cost penalty (50% more) when placed over existing buildings
- Versatile enhancement tool for optimizing tile productivity
- Essential for maximizing resource generation from key locations
