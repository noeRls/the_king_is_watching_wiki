Dragons' Bakery
===============

Cycle: 59s

Function/Description:
Bakes Flour Dragons into Ash Dragons.

Build Costs:
- Wood: 10
- Coal: 50

Population Requirements:
Not specified in the screenshot

Special Abilities/Bonuses/Effects:
- Production speed: Boosts production by 30%
- Magic scales: Receive 100 crystals after baking a Dragon

Additional Notes:
- Specialized building for dragon transformation/enhancement
- Converts Flour Dragons (white dragons) into Ash Dragons (black dragons)
- Provides significant crystal income through the magic scales bonus
- The production speed boost applies to the baking process
