MILITIA CAMP

Building Information:
- Cycle: 15s
- Capacity: 3

Requirements:
- Create one unit per cycle
- Decreases resources by 12 wheat and 15 wood

Function: Recruits militia

Build Cost:
- 120 wood
- 30 wheat
- 30 gold

MILITIA UNIT STATS:

Base Characteristics:
- HP: 140
- Damage per second: 7
- Unit Type: [Warrior] (red circular icon)

Unit Trait:
+2% to HP of Militias for each Militia on the battlefield.

UPGRADES:

Militia HP:
- Increases militia HP by 50%

Militia Capacity:
- Increases capacity by 2

Mega Dude:
- Produces a MEGA militia after recruiting 4 base troops
